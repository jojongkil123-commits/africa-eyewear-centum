About the store

AFRICA EYEWEAR is an optician located inside Shinsegae Centum City, Busan's landmark department store, on Basement Level 2 (B2). We carry authentic Ray-Ban, Oakley, Miu Miu and other premium designer sunglasses and eyeglasses, with on-site optometry, prescription lenses and frame fitting.

It's a convenient stop for travelers looking to buy sunglasses or glasses in Busan — just a 2-minute walk from Centum City Station on Metro Line 2. Open Mon–Thu 10:30–20:00, Fri–Sun until 20:30.
